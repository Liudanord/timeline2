2005

Year When I Decided I Never Become an Artist

There was my first step to the creative field: I was 9 and I entered local art school, I loved drawing since my very young age and for the first time I was very excited. But after several months my enthusiasm disappeared. There was 60 years date of the ww2 victory in that year. And all the students had to draw only about war and in general all the rules were very strict, technics and materials always were the same.It lasted for 6 months, I was 9 years old girl and I was tired of it very soon, I wanted doing something different, wanted to experiment, wanted to create cool stuff. But all I was given, were war, death and acrylic paint. There was time of studentâ€™s exhibition, and I was too bored with all those absolutely similar pictures my classmates drew. But In was already rebellious I wanted to go different, and decided do something that will shock. And I did. I drew the a1 picture with the angel flying over the city, but the Angel was in SS soldier uniform. My plan had worked my Nazi-Angel was a bomb! My teacher was shocked and mad about it, to be honest. Of course they did not take my picture to the exhibition, and very soon I was kicked out from the art school. In two words 2005 was the year I decided never draw again.
